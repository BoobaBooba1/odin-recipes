Grilled Lettuce

It's bloody awful!

This is a first for me... a grilled ceasar salad... with grilled lettuce. A show of hands of anyone else whos had this?

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Chipotle sauce
  • Salt & Pepper

Directions

  1. Put lettuce on grill at high heat
  2. Grill until golden brown on bottom
  3. Season with salt & pepper
  4. Add chipotle sauce
  5. Profit
